Planting trees is one of the most environment-friendly activities we can do. Trees help clean the air by absorbing harmful carbon dioxide and giving us fresh oxygen to breathe. FAQ

+

1. Why should we plant trees?

Trees give us oxygen, reduce air pollution, provide shade, improve soil, and support wildlife.

+

2. How do trees help in good health?

They clean the air we breathe, provide fruits and medicines, reduce stress, and create a healthier environment.

+

3. Which trees are best to plant?

It is best to plant native trees like neem, mango, banyan, peepal, jamun, amla, and moringa, as they suit the local climate and need less care.

+

4. When is the best time to plant trees?

The rainy season (monsoon) is the best time because young trees need plenty of water to grow.
